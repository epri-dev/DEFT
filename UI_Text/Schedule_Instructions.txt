ONLY include vehicles that are currently fueled but will be converted to a zero-emissions alternative.

The DEFT collects an "average day" schedule and a "busy day" schedule.

The average day is used to calculate changes in yearly electricity use, fuel savings and time of day energy charges.

The busy day is meant to represent the busiest the terminal will be in any month and is used to calculate the increase in peak load and demand charges.
