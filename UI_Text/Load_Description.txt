Click "Upload Baseline Load Data" to upload an already-formatted csv file containing baseline electric load profile.

OR

Click "Upload Utility Bills" to upload information from your utility bills to estimate your load profile.

If you know the maximum load that the your connection can serve, enter it in the 'planned load limit' box.

After uploading the file, you can preview the data by interacting with the plot to the right. You can pan, zoom, scroll, etc.
